Breadcrumbs

The breadcrumbs pattern is an ordered list with $base-border-style and left and right padding that follow the $spacing-sm spacing rules. Its height is configured by $breadcrumbs-height.

Each item in the breadcrumb list has a max width controlled by $breadcrumbs-max-width (75px). Text that extends beyond this limit will be ellipsed.

The exceptions to the max width are the first item in the list (if it is the brand logo) and the last item in the list, which is allowed to fill available space.
